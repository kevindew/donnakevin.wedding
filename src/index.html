How to get there

<%= svgCore.icon({ iconName: 'car' }, { classes: ['icon-guide__icon'] }).html %>

Car

For sat navs the postcode is HG3 1JH, otherwise directions are available from Google Maps or Rudding Park.

Once near the venue be careful to take the turning signposted to the hotel as there are different entrances for the golf course and holiday park that share Rudding Park.

There is ample parking at the venue.

<%= svgCore.icon({ iconName: 'subway' }, { classes: ['icon-guide__icon'] }).html %>

Public transport

The venue cannot be reached directly by public transport. We recommend travelling by train to Harrogate and using a taxi to reach the venue, which is a 15 minute drive and should cost approximately £10.

Trains from London depart from Kings Cross. Tickets can be purchased from lner.co.uk, they tend to be available 3 months before the travel date and are cheaper the earlier you book.

<%= svgCore.icon({ iconName: 'taxi' }, { classes: ['icon-guide__icon'] }).html %>

Local Taxis

We have been recommend Blue Line Taxis as a local firm that regularly serve the venue, they can be contacted on 01423 530830.

We recommend you pre-book with them if you need a taxi for after the wedding.

Places to stay

Rudding Park

Stay with us at Rudding Park Hotel & Spa and be onsite for the whole occasion. You can even extend your stay to enjoy a spa or golf break. Rooms here are on the more expensive side but it is a lovely place to stay and very convenient.

Premier Inn

Less than 10 minutes drive from Rudding Park is Premier Inn Harrogate South which offers a more economical accommodation choice. It has good parking facilities and is right next to Hornbeam Park station.

Harrogate

You can make the occasion more of a city break by staying in Harrogate town centre which offers easy access to rail services and is a 15 minute taxi ride from Rudding Park.

There are plenty of accommodation options available that should suit all budgets.

On the day

11:30 Arrival

You can start arriving at Rudding Park from 11:30, if you head towards the reception there will be people there to direct you to the event. We’ll need bums on seats by 12:15 for the ceremony.

12:30 Ceremony

The big moment! We will be having a relaxed civil ceremony in the Radcliffe room of Rudding House.

13:00 Reception

Time to throw some confetti and drink some fizz, we will be providing both. This is a chance for some photos and some mingling. There will also be a kids room and games to keep the little (and not so little) ones entertained.

15:00 Breakfast

Everyone will be sitting down for a 3 course meal, followed by speeches.

18:30 Party

Witness the cutting of the cake and our first dance. A DJ will encourage everyone to hit the dance floor and throw some shapes.

20:00 Buffet

Hungry? Never fear we will be providing some finger food for everyone and there will also be plenty of cake.

01:00 Home time

The music will stop at 1 - well done if you manage to party until the end.

Coronavirus

We want everyone to enjoy themselves safely at our wedding, we therefore ask guests to take responsible steps to reduce the risks of spreading the virus.

We ask attendees to:

  • follow the venue's coronavirus policy, which includes wearing a mask when moving around the venue and a temperature check on arrival
  • be considerate towards other guests - who may be taking a cautious approach to contact - and provide them with adequate space
  • consider taking a rapid flow test before attending
  • not attend if they have recently tested positive for coronavirus or are experiencing any symptoms

If the weather is suitable we will make use of outdoor space for some of the social parts of the day. The indoor aspects of the day will take place in large and well ventilated rooms.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
