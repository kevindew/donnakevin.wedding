How to get there

<%= svgCore.icon({ iconName: 'car' }, { classes: ['icon-guide__icon'] }).html %>

Car

Car is the easiest way to get to Rudding Park and there is plenty of parking at the venue. Rudding park is just off the A658. Take J47 off the A1 onto A59 to reach the A658. The venue is really well signposted.

<%= svgCore.icon({ iconName: 'subway' }, { classes: ['icon-guide__icon'] }).html %>

Public transport

The nearest station to Rudding Park is Panal and it is just a 5 minute taxi ride from there.

Alternatively you can head to Harrogate station, the venue is a 13 minute taxi ride from there.

We have found that booking trains in advance is much cheaper, especially if you are travelling from London. Book your tickets here: lner.co.uk
